About Stoink

Stoink for Stock Price Prediction is a system that uses deep neural network(DNN) to predict the price increase or decrease in percentage of one or more stocks for the next 1-3 months.

Deep Neural Network (DNN)

The Deep Neural Network is based on RNN (Recurrent Neural Network) structure, which can remember each information throughout time series in predicting stock prices.

The stocks
